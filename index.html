Welcome to Thailand, the land of Smiles. We are pleased to hear that you are thinking about wanting to travel here one day to our beautiful country. We are home to some of the most majestic places as well as a buzzing nightlife. We have:

  • Good Things for Cheap: Most things in Thailand are more cheaper then places around the world, however we still bring the same great effort other places have(Ex:Fashionable Haircuts for only $2USD!)
  • Great Culture:We are full of many cultures in out country, especially in Buddist culture. We also have many festivals celebrating many of our cultures and our lifestyle
  • Stuuning Beaches: We are home to spectacular beaches located in southern Thailand. We offer a whole host of main land and island beaches that are just waiting to be explored. From popular spots like Koh Phi Phi, to the underated spots in Koh Yao Noi, Thailand has a beach for everyone.

The flag of Thailand is made of horizontal stripes of red, white, blue, white, and red. Red symbolizes the blood the Thai people have shed for Thailand, white stands for the the religious laws and the purity of Buddhism, and Blue stands for the royal family.

Thailand's national sport s Muay Thai, which is a combat sport that originated in the 16th century. It is kown as the "art of eight limbs".

Thailand is home to over 40,000 temples. Over 30,000 of these years are still used in by Buddhist monks.
